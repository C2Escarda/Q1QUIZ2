Miles Morales , A 13 year old Graffiti Artist gets bitten by a Radioactive Spider and gets involved in a scene with Spider-Man and the Green Goblin.
Given a task to keep a USB safe, Miles runs away from the fight as Spider-Man dies to protect him. To honor Spider-Man ,
Miles uses his new found powers to finish what Spider-Man left behind and become the new Spider-Man.
